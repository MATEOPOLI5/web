XILITLA

Xilitla se al tepetl ika costumbres tlen wejkaya ewanin tlen nahuaswan tenek, tlen istoken sentik ka kuititlamitl wan yankuik tonali, itstok ipan se wey tepetl tlen huasteca ... tlen mo pantia wan yejyektsin tlali tlen kin paktia paxalowanin, para tijkixmatis kipia tikitas tijmatis, tijnenemis ipan ojtli, ti paxalos kampa tla yejyetsin timokahuas mits yowalos mixtle tlen kemantik kitsacua totlal.

E. James Sculpture Garden, Las Pozas

"Las Pozas" is a set of architectural structures made of concrete and fantastic routes that make up a sculpture garden crossed by a river with waterfalls and surrounded by the Potosi jungle on an extensive land.
